Runa Card is a loyalty platform that helps businesses turn repeat visits into rewards. Customers save their points card directly in Apple Wallet or Google Wallet—no extra app to download—and earn points every time they return.

## Features

- Apple Wallet and Google Wallet support
- Web pass view for customers without a wallet app
- Real-time point updates with automatic notifications
- Custom branding—colors, logo, and reward rules
- Simple web dashboard to manage customers, points, and rewards
- Assisted onboarding to get your program live in under 24 hours

## How It Works

Businesses configure their rewards program from a control panel, then share a QR code or link so customers can add their card to their wallet. Each purchase earns points that update instantly on the customer's card. When they reach a goal, they redeem the reward you define—like a free coffee after 10 visits.

## Why Use Runa Card?

If you run a café, restaurant, spa, retail shop, or any business where customers come back, Runa Card makes loyalty simple:

- No app friction for your customers
- Native wallet experience on iPhone and Android
- Launch a full rewards program quickly
- Increase retention with point-based incentives
